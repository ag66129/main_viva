INDIA-FILM-PROJECT

India Film Project is an annual content creation festival. The festival features a flagship 24 Hour filmmaking challenge, where participants are given 24 hours to make a film after which a panel of judges select award winners. Competitors form teams and are given a common theme to which they then write a script and shoot the films. They have a free choice of locations, equipment and actors. By participating in this competition , you adhere to the criteria as the sole creditor for the movie.

Rules:- 1. The theme will be revealed via WhatsApp/SMS at the beginning of 24 hours. 2. Tasks-Each team has to perform following tasks in the given period of 24 hours – Scripting , Casting , Location and Art Design. Teams can shoot on DSLR or Mobile on 720p or higher. However , the editing must be done on laptop. 4. Team leaders will receive the link for uploading the films at the start of the competition. 5. Maximum length of the film should be 120 seconds excluding beginning and end credits. 6. Stock audios used should be mentioned in the end . 7. You can have maximum 10 members in your team, excluding the cast. 8. Any film received after the 24 hours deadline will be disqualified Judging Criteria:- 1.Direction 2.Acting 3.Editing 4.Cinematography 5.Story 6.Script 7.Audio
